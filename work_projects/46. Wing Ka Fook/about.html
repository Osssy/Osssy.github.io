How to make a bulk order?

First you need to decide on the goods. If you have any difficulties in choosing, you can use the advice of our managers. Next, you should choose the payment and delivery methods that suit you. You can use both cash and non-cash payments.

What is the product warranty?

Upon receipt of the goods, you can inspect the order and, if something does not suit you, send the order back, indicating the problems. If the product does not correspond to the declared indicators or is defective, we are ready to make a warranty replacement.

What are the delivery times?

The duration of delivery depends on the choice of the type of delivery. (the fastest is air delivery). It also depends on the country to which we will make the delivery (this affects the duration of resolving customs issues).

What are the security guarantees for the client?

The client has all the rights provided for by the laws of China. We observe them and act strictly according to the law during disputes. Therefore, we can guarantee an adequate attitude from our side.
