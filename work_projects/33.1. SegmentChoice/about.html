SegmentChoice

Provides the best quality products and services

SegmentChoice is portuguese company that already has enough experience in the construction business. We are both wholesalers of goods and sellers of services.

Our approach is simple. By selecting SegmentChoice early in the project preconstruction stage, we are able to work collaboratively with the owner and the design team to meet the project objectives while maintaining control of the scope, budget, and schedule. We are also able to enlist the support of trusted subcontractors for pricing and design assistance.

This project approach of pre-selection and early involvement of Holland is a valuable approach for any construction project, whether the contracted delivery method is construction manager as adviser, construction manager as constructor, design-builder, or contractor. With Holland, you will have the peace of mind of knowing that your project was completed with the greatest attention to detail, with no short-cuts or surprises, and with the highest level of personal and corporate integrity.

If you need quality products for construction on the most favorable conditions, SegmentChoice - is the right choice. We sell products only from time-tested suppliers and guarantee the best conditions.

We are ready to cooperate with construction companies of various levels and scales. We deliver the goods under the conditions prescribed in the contract. All construction services are also secured by an official contract and begin in time. You'll be satisfied with the cooperation with us. Join us as soon as possible.
