About Setra Business DOO

We are young company with great ambitions, active employees and ambitious goals.

Setra Business DOO, as one of the largest platforms facilitating trade amongst international suppliers and purchasers of commodities, now offers every importer and purchaser the sanguine opportunity to procure electrical supplies and equipment at the most economical rates, and grow alongside the industry that is not expected to slow down any time soon.

Since its foundation, we have been constantly making improvements to customer service and have already been able to compete with more experienced firms.

The enormous growth in the prevalence of electricity has been inexorable. The need for electrical equipment and supplies all over the globe is now more than ever. Nonetheless, as the demands have increased, suppliers have too.

We are engaged in wholesale sales of building materials, timber, plumbing equipment, industrial electrical equipment, as well as provide construction and installation services and electrical installation
