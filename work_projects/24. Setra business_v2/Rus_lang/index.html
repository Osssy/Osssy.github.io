The most sought after building materials

The market for building materials is huge: every year new technologies and products appear, while old, time-tested, do not disappear. However, the five leaders include the same products year after year: boards, bricks and decking....

  1. Board – one of the oldest lumber, and one of the most popular. Over the course of hundreds of years, many species have been invented, but today edged boards are most in demand - that is, products about all six machined surfaces.
  2. Brick – another oldest building material known to mankind since the heyday of Ancient Egypt. Today, more than a thousand types of bricks are known, but the most popular in modern construction are silicate and clay hollow and solid.
  3. Profiled Flooring, as a unified building material, it is used everywhere today: in floor coverings, on fences, gates, siding of houses and, of course, for roofing.

Building Material Tips

As for finishing materials, it is easy to choose them yourself, focusing on your preferences and tastes. But if you need building materials for complex work, you should contact a specialist who will give valuable advice and help you choose the necessary equipment....

  1. To repair was durable, do not save on materials, good quality implies an equivalent cost. If you buy cheap concrete, then it may not be strong enough, which will lead to unnecessary costs.
  2. Also, do not chase the high price, many expensive products are produced by brands that use their fame, but do not always monitor the quality of their products.
  3. Check the packaging for safety. If it is damaged, this means that the storage conditions were not met. This is especially true for putty and concrete mixes.
  4. It is better to purchase building materials in specialized stores, there the probability of buying a good product at an optimal cost is significantly increased.
  5. Sometimes in stores, sales of stock balances in warehouses are arranged. By purchasing such a product, you can save a lot on repair.

Electrical equipment of industrial buildings

The electrical equipment of industrial buildings is a complex of electrical systems mounted in industrial buildings in order to ensure the smooth operation of units and lighting fixtures necessary for an uninterrupted production process. The electrical equipment of industrial buildings performs another important function - ensuring the safety of employees....

Electrical equipment for industrial buildings include:

  1. aggregates of the electrical principle;
  2. apparatuses and devices of the power supply network, guaranteeing the proper functioning of artificial lighting, household electrical appliances; ventilation, heating systems and water supply systems;
  3. industrial vehicles.

Installation of industrial electrical equipment
