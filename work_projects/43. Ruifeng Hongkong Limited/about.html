About Us

Ruifen HongKong Limited

We are a modern high-tech company that is engaged in the wholesale of various product groups throughout Asia. The main products are electronics and computers, we also supply components and spare parts, software for electronics, various gadgets and modern digital devices. In 2021, we started selling building materials of various applications and categories, we are also engaged in the supply of timber and lumber and sanitary equipment.

We invite investors, suppliers, customers, employees to cooperate. Join us to be part of a successful project. The future belongs to us!
