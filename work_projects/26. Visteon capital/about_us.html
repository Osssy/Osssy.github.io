About Us

«Visteon Capital»

Wholesale of: automotive parts, components and accessories; electrical parts and accessories; industrial electrical equipment, apparatus and machinery; sanitary-technical equipment; building materials; timber.

Despite the recent opening, we are a modern, dynamically developing company that is going to win the recognition and trust of consumers, become an official dealer and distributor of many well-known manufacturers. We're planning to have partnerships with a number of large companies. We are also constantly working to increase similar business contacts in our sales areas.

Collaboration with suppliers without intermediaries gives us the opportunity to create a low, flexible pricing policy for customers. Our company has professional staff (we are also interested in hiring). We provide each client with the necessary advice on the product range, technical aspects, and we carry out the full range of work on paperwork and order delivery.

We focus on long-term cooperation and build our relations with business partners on mutually beneficial conditions. We value everything we have today and believe that tomorrow for our company will be more productive, perfect and successful!
